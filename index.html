BRYSON SHARE BOOST FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about our automated Facebook share boosting service.

What is Bryson Share Boost?

Bryson Share Boost is an automated service that helps increase the visibility of your Facebook posts by automatically sharing them multiple times using your Facebook account credentials (via appstate). It's designed to help content creators, marketers, and businesses amplify their reach on Facebook.

Is this service safe to use?

While we've implemented measures to make the sharing process as safe as possible, automated interactions with Facebook always carry some risk. We recommend:

  • Using reasonable share limits (don't set it too high)
  • Adding delays between shares
  • Not using your primary Facebook account
  • Monitoring your account for any unusual activity

What is Facebook appstate?

Appstate is a string that contains your Facebook session cookies. It allows our service to interact with Facebook on your behalf without needing your actual password. You can get your appstate by following our tutorial guide (available in the menu).

Important: Treat your appstate as sensitive information, similar to your password. Never share it with anyone you don't trust.

How do I set the optimal share limit?

The optimal share limit depends on several factors:

  • New accounts: Start with 10-20 shares per day
  • Established accounts: 30-50 shares per day is generally safe
  • Very active accounts: Up to 100 shares may be possible

We recommend starting with a low number and gradually increasing while monitoring your account's status. Using delays between shares (500-2000ms) can also help avoid detection.

Why isn't my sharing process starting?

If your sharing process isn't starting, check these common issues:

  1. Ensure your appstate is valid and not expired (get a fresh one if needed)
  2. Make sure the Facebook post URL is correct and accessible
  3. Check that you've entered a share limit number
  4. Verify all required checkboxes are enabled
  5. Try refreshing the page and submitting again

If problems persist, contact our admin through the menu.

How can I check my share history?

Your share history is available in the side menu under "Share History." For security, this section is password protected. The default password is "***********" (without quotes).

Your history shows all your previous share attempts with their status and completion counts.

Can I share to multiple groups or just my timeline?

Currently, Bryson Share Boost only supports sharing to your own timeline. Sharing to groups would require additional permissions and carries higher risk of account restrictions.

For timeline shares, the visibility will depend on your post's privacy settings (Public, Friends, etc.).

What should I do if my account gets restricted?

If Facebook restricts your account:

  1. Stop all automated sharing immediately
  2. Follow Facebook's instructions to verify your account
  3. Wait several days before attempting any automated actions
  4. When resuming, use much lower share limits and longer delays
  5. Consider using a different account if restrictions persist

Remember that account restrictions are ultimately Facebook's decision, and we cannot guarantee account safety when using automation tools.

Is there a way to schedule shares for later?

Currently, Bryson Share Boost doesn't support scheduling. Shares begin immediately when you click "Boost Now." However, you can simulate scheduling by:

  • Setting a high delay between shares (e.g., 3600000ms = 1 hour)
  • Starting the process at your desired time
  • Letting it run slowly over an extended period

Future versions may include proper scheduling functionality.
